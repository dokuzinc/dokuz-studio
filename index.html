ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Dokuz. I'm a versatile designer and developer who blends creativity with the power of technology. By day, I work in the fast-paced world of art direction; by night, I dive into game development, AI-generated visuals, and web projects.

I have a strong interest in graphic design, UI/UX, 3D modeling, and cinematic visual aesthetics. I develop multiplayer games with Unity and create interactive 3D experiences on the web using Three.js. In my projects, I prioritize both visual detail and user experience.

I also enjoy staying creative through code—building Discord bots, mobile games, AI-powered visual systems, and animated web interfaces. I actively develop projects using languages like C#, C++, JavaScript, and Node.js.

In my spare time, I love creating cinematic-style photography, experimenting with analog film effects, and exploring new creative ideas. Sometimes, I just unwind with a good series or a fun game.

My multidisciplinary nature allows me to balance both technical and artistic skills, and I’m always eager to keep learning and evolving.
